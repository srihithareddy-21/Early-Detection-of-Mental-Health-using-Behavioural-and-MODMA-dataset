Multimodal AI · Research Tool

Multimodal Depression
Screening System

Combining acoustic voice biomarkers with structured behavioral questionnaires via late fusion — a research-grade screening pipeline built on clinical evidence.

🎙️

Voice Analysis

Extracts MFCCs, ZCR, RMS energy, and delta-MFCCs from raw speech. A CNN-BiLSTM model maps acoustic features to depression probability.

📋

Behavioral Questionnaire

23 validated questions across Sleep, Physical, Work, Social, Mood, History, and Motivation domains.

⚗️

Late Fusion Engine

Weighted combination of audio score (α=0.6) and behavioral score (β=0.4) with sigmoid calibration and confidence estimation.

⚠️ Clinical Disclaimer NeuroScreen is a research and educational tool only. It is not FDA-cleared or validated for clinical diagnosis. Results must not be used to make clinical decisions without oversight from a qualified mental health professional.
